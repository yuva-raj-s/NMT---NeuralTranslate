Key Features

Our translation system combines neural machine learning with robust evaluation metrics to provide high-quality translations across Asian languages.

13 Asian Languages

Support for Bengali, Filipino, Hindi, Indonesian, Japanese, Khmer, Lao, Malay, Myanmar, Thai, Vietnamese, and Chinese - all optimized for the unique characteristics of Asian languages.

Neural Technology

Powered by advanced mBART neural machine translation models trained with the ALT dataset for superior accuracy. Our system understands context and linguistic nuances for more natural translations.

Quality Metrics

Every translation includes BLEU, ROUGE, and METEOR evaluation metrics - the industry standards for assessing translation quality, giving you confidence in the accuracy of your translations.

Meet our interactive language mascot that guides you through the translation process. It provides helpful tips, reacts to your actions, and displays language-specific personalities for an engaging experience.

Neural Animation
